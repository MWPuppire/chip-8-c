All controls are configurable
the actual CHIP-8 had 16 hex-keys
default 1, 2, 3, 4
	Q, W, E, R
	A, S, D, F
	Z, X, C, V

Special controls
	rapid fire - every update, held buttons are "pressed" again
	quick save, quick load
	mute
	pause/resume
	rewind, slow motion, fast forward - configurable speeds
	step backward, step forward - exactly one instruction
	screenshot, record video
	debug mode
